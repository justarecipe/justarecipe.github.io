Time: 5 minutes; Yields: 2 small smoothies 

_Frozen cherries and cocoa powder swirl together for an healthy cool treat._

INGREDIENTS

1 cup frozen cherries

1 cup milk or non-dairy milk

1\.5 Tbsp. cocoa powder

1 tsp. vanilla

3/4 cup ice
